General Entry Requirements

Ensure you meet these benchmarks before picking up your application form.

1

Ordinary Level Entry (O'Level)

Candidates must possess 5 Credit passes in SSCE / GCE / NECO / NABTEB in relevant subjects at not more than 2 sittings.

2

Direct Entry Requirements

Credit/Merit in 2 subjects at OND / NCE / IJMB / A'Level and 5 Credit passes including English Language & Mathematics in SSCE / GCE / NECO / NABTEB in relevant subjects at not more than 2 sittings.
