About McDonalds & BOUESTI

Bringing standard accredited degree programmes from Bamidele Olumilua University of Education, Science and Technology, Ikere-Ekiti (BOUESTI) directly to learners in Ibadan [1].

Our Foundation

Empowering Student Career Journeys Through Flexible Education

McDonalds Educational Services is proud to partner with the renowned Bamidele Olumilua University of Education, Science and Technology, Ikere-Ekiti (BOUESTI) as an approved Directorate Study Centre [1]. Our primary mission is to expand access to high-quality higher education for students who require flexible educational arrangements [1].

By coordinating weekend part-time programs directly at the Ibadan study center, we make it possible for working professionals, entrepreneurs, and busy students to earn standard, accredited university degrees without having to relocate to the main campus in Ekiti [1].
